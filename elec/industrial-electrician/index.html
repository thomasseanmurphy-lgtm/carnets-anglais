📖 The story

Read me first!

Marta is an industrial electrician in a large factory. Every morning, she checks the main switchboard and tests the circuit breakers. She always wears gloves and safety boots, because the panels carry a high voltage. If a machine loses power, the whole production line stops.

Last week, the team installed a new control panel. First, Marta cut the power and locked the switch. Then she connected the wires and tightened each terminal. Finally, an engineer measured the voltage and signed the report.

Since Monday, Marta has replaced four old fuses and has tested every socket. She has not found any fault yet, and the line has run without a single stop. Her supervisor is very happy! 🔧
