📖 The story

Read me first!

Théo works as a maintenance technician in a large factory. Every week, he plans the servicing of the machines and supervises the team. Today is special: a new hydraulic press arrives, and he must oversee its installation.

This morning, two trucks delivered the press in several parts. A mobile crane lifted the heaviest part — nearly twelve tonnes — over the wall and lowered it slowly onto the concrete base. The riggers attached the load with steel slings and guided it into position.

Since then, the team has bolted the machine to the floor and has checked the alignment twice. They haven't started the commissioning yet, but everything is on schedule. Théo has already signed the safety checklist.

During the lift, Théo talked with the foreman and the crane operator:

Foreman: "The load is very heavy — over ten tonnes."
Théo: "We must check the slings before we lift."
Operator: "I can lift it now."
Théo: "Wait — the area isn't clear yet."
Foreman: "OK, the machine is in position."
Théo: "Good. We have finished the alignment."

By the end of the day, the press was ready for testing. 🏗️
