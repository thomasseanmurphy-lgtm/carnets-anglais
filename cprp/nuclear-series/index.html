📖 The story

Read me first!

Our workshop makes metal parts. These parts go to the nuclear industry, so they must be very precise. Every day, our CNC machines cut steel.

Last Monday, the team started a new series. First, the programmer wrote the program. Then the operator put a metal bar in the machine. The machine cut the first part in ten minutes. The quality team measured it. The part was perfect.

Since Monday, we have made 300 parts. We have checked every part with a gauge. We have not had any problem. The client is happy! 🎉
