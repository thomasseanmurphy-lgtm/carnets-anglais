📖 The story

Read me first!

Nadia is a logistics manager in Dijon. Every day, her drivers deliver goods to twenty shops in the region. The current route is not optimized: the trucks drive too many kilometers, they often come back empty, and two drivers sometimes visit the same area. Nadia knows the company loses time and fuel.

Last week, Nadia analyzed the delivery data. She mapped every stop, measured the distances and tested a new route on the computer. She also called the drivers and asked for their opinion. The new plan grouped the shops by zone and removed the empty return trips.

Since Monday, the new route has reduced the distance by twenty percent and has saved fuel. But one important client, a supermarket, hasn't accepted the new delivery time yet. Nadia has called the manager to negotiate: she wants to keep the client happy without breaking the whole schedule. 🚚
